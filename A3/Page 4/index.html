The Paradox Ending

In this ending, the choices you've made during your time-traveling adventures have caused significant disruptions in the fabric of time. The consequences of your actions have led to a paradox, a tangled web of events that defy explanation. When you return to the present, you are met with a world in chaos. Historical figures are out of place, critical moments in history have been altered beyond recognition, and you realize that the continuum of time has been irreparably damaged.

You find yourself grappling with the weight of the paradox you've created, a haunting reminder of the unforeseen consequences of your choices. Your once-familiar world has become a strange and unpredictable place, and you must now navigate this disordered reality, searching for a way to restore the natural flow of time or to live with the chaos you've inadvertently unleashed.
